The UI must feel warm, calm, and safe — not clinical or robotic:
- Use soft, muted color palette (no harsh reds or aggressive colors)
- Micro-animations on AI responses (gentle fade-in, not instant pop)
- Conversational tone in all UI copy — no corporate/medical language

- Empty states must be encouraging, not blank
